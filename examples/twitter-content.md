# Example: Twitter Content Question

## User Question

```text
创始人如何在 Twitter 上做爆款内容？
```

## Expected Style

The skill should score this as a low-to-mid quality question, not a high-quality strategic question.

Expected diagnosis:

- The question is still action-level.
- It treats "viral content" as the goal.
- It lacks target audience, business model, conversion path, and validation criteria.
- It should be upgraded from "how to go viral" to "how founder content serves commercial distribution, trust, and positioning."

## Upgraded Direction

```text
创始人应该持续输出哪一类行业判断，使目标客户在理解问题时自然采用我的框架，并因此更容易信任我的产品或服务？
```

## Evaluation Notes

A good answer should distinguish:

- traffic versus business asset
- viral content versus qualified attention
- founder IP versus company positioning
- platform distribution versus commercial conversion
- content tactics versus problem-explanation rights
